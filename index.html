Revolutionizing Hospital Efficiency and Patient Care with MediQueue

MediQueue is a cutting-edge solution designed to drastically reduce waiting times in the healthcare landscape while simultaneously reducing workload and improving working conditions for medical professionals.

Get Started

Our solution

Are you also tired of the struggles of todays primary care? Getting an appointment, treatment and right answers should not take hours over hours of your time. Using MediQueues' cutting-edge technology, we not only resolved all of these issues within a single application but also made it available for free.

Reducing Wait Times

As per Sweden's National Board of Health and Welfare (Socialstyrelsen), the typical emergency room waiting time in Sweden can extend to 8 hours, with a failed target of reducing it to 3 hours. MediQ is designed to ensure a maximum inside hospital waiting time of 1 hour, granting individuals the flexibility to select their preferred waiting area as well.

Empowering healthcare providers

Through MediQ we are empowering medical staff to concentrate on critically important tasks, resulting in a working environment that is markedly less stressful and more appealing. This, in turn, allows them to prioritize patient care over administrative duties, fostering a more engaging and fulfilling atmosphere for medical professionals.

This is what MediQ can achieve

  • Time savings per visit (patient) : 210-440 min
  • Time saved for staff per day: ≈ 600 min
  • Costs saved per day (hospital): ≈ 2000 sek
